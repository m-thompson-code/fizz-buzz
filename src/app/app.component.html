Fizz Buzz

Fizz Buzz is a classic game where players take turns "counting" from numbers with some special conditions. Players are eliminated if he or she hesitates or makes a mistake. Game concludes when there is one player left. [source]

  2. If the number is divisible by 5, map to "Buzz"
